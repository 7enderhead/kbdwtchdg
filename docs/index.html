Welcome to kbdwtchdg’s documentation!¶

Definition¶

V-USB-based watchdog that identifies itself as a USB keyboard and sends keyboard strokes.

What can kbdwtchdg do?¶

It sends a defined message via USB to the computer. It does that either after a specific period of time after being plugged in, or after a capslock trigger (pressing capslock x times).
